About LifeChain

LifeChain started with a simple idea: safety should be easy, affordable, and accessible. Our vision is to empower individuals worldwide with wearable emergency technology that instantly alerts loved ones. We are a group of students from Stonehill International School, Bangalore, who has a mission, and a plan to create something great

Our Story

Founded by tech enthusiasts who wanted to make safety wearable and reliable for everyone.

Our Vision

To create a connected world where emergency help is just a button press away.

Our Values

Accessibility, reliability, and user-friendly design guide everything we do.
